Passive:

Savagery

Rengar's next two basic attacks within 3 seconds gain 40% bonus attack speed, with the first also becoming an unstoppable basic attack, gaining 25 bonus range and dealing bonus physical damage.

Empowered: Savagery's bonus physical damage is modified and grants Rengar bonus attack speed for 5 seconds.


(hover on the image to see the details)

Battle Roar

Rengar lets out a battle roar, dealing magic damage to nearby enemies and healing himself for 50% of the damage he's taken in the last 1.5 seconds. This is 75% effective vs monsters.

Empowered: Battle Roar's magic damage is modified and additionally cleanses Rengar of all Stun icon crowd control.


(hover on the image to see the details)

Bola Strike

Rengar throws a bola in the target direction, dealing physical damage to the first enemy hit and slowing them for 1.75 seconds.

Empowered: Bola Strike's physical damage is modified and roots its target for 1.75 seconds.


(hover on the image to see the details)

Thrill of the Hunt

Rengar activates his predatory instincts, revealing the nearest enemy champion and gaining

bonus movement speed

for a duration. After

2 seconds

, he also becomes

camouflaged

and gains

Unseen Predator

for the remaining duration. His next basic attack within normal attack range against any enemy unit or against the nearest enemy champion deals

50% bonus physical damage

and reduces the target's

armor

for 4 seconds. The nearest enemy champion to Rengar alerts all of their allies within 1200 range about his presence, and they can sense him at 1600 range. Attacking or casting abilities, though not Savagery, ends Thrill of the Hunt immediately.
